Example-01: Make a decision tree for the following dataset

Source: Decision Tree Tutorial Video
Day Weather Temperature Humidity Wind Play Football
Day_1 Sunny Hot High Weak No
Day_2 Sunny Hot High Strong No
Day_3 Cloudy Hot High Weak Yes
Day_14 Rain Mild High Strong No

Solution

Initial Calculations

Total number of Sunny Days = 5

Total number of Cloudy Days = 4

Total number of Rainy Days = 5

Total = 14

Total Positive ("Yes") = 9

Total Negative ("No") = 5

Calculate IG (Information Gain) of Weather

Step 1: Entropy of entire Dataset

Total Entropy(S) = -(p₊)log₂(p₊) - (p₋)log₂(p₋)

Where:

  • p₊ = Positive Class Proportion ("Yes") = 9/14
  • p₋ = Negative Class Proportion ("No") = 5/14

S{+9,-5} = -9/14 log₂(9/14) - 5/14 log₂(5/14) = 0.94

Step 2: Entropy of All Attributes

Weather Part

Entropy of Sunny:

Entropy(Sunny){+2,-3} = -2/5 log₂(2/5) - 3/5 log₂(3/5) = 0.97

Entropy of Cloudy:

Entropy(Cloudy){+4,-0} = -4/4 log₂(4/4) - 0/4 log₂(0/4) = 0

Entropy of Rain:

Entropy(Rain){+3,-2} = -3/5 log₂(3/5) - 2/5 log₂(2/5) = 0.97

Information Gain:

IG(Weather) = 0.94 - (5/14)(0.97) - (4/14)(0) - (5/14)(0.97) = 0.246

Now we have:

  • IG(S, weather) = 0.246
  • IG(S, temperature) = 0.029
  • IG(S, humidity) = 0.15
  • IG(S, wind) = 0.0478

IGmax (max of four) = 0.246 (weather)

So, Weather will be the root node
